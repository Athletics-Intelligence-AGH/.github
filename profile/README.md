## Who are we?

We are a community of AGH University students passionate about bringing cutting-edge technology into the world of sport. We combine a love for sports with advanced analytics, artificial intelligence, and data science — because we believe that data and algorithms can become a genuine competitive advantage.

---

## 🎯 What we do

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 🤖 **AI / ML** | Predictive models, classification, reinforcement learning applied to sport |
| 👁️ **Computer Vision** | Player tracking, match video analysis, pose estimation |
| 📊 **Data Science** | Sports data exploration, dashboards & interactive visualizations |
| 🏃 **Sport Analytics** | Match statistics, biometric data and wearable sensor processing |
